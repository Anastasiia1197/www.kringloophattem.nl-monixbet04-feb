Privacy Policy – Your Data, Clearly Protected

At https://www.cjcustomdesign.co.nz/, your privacy is a cornerstone of our service. This Privacy Policy outlines our clear and unwavering commitment to protecting your personal information. We believe in complete transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. Our practices are designed not only to comply with applicable privacy laws but to earn your trust. We process your information to deliver a secure, efficient, and personalised gaming experience tailored for New Zealand players. By using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

This document explains what information we gather, why it's necessary, and the rights you have over it. We encourage you to read it carefully. Our goal is to empower you with knowledge, ensuring you feel confident and secure whenever you interact with our services.

What data we collect and how it powers your experience

To provide our services effectively, we collect necessary information that falls into several categories:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect details such as your name, email address, date of birth, and chosen username. This data is essential for creating and securing your account, verifying your identity (as required by law), and communicating with you.
  • Financial & Transaction Data: To process deposits and withdrawals, we collect payment method details (e.g., masked card numbers, e-wallet IDs) and a full history of your transactions. This is critical for financial integrity, fraud prevention, and resolving any payment-related queries.
  • Technical & Usage Data: Automatically, we collect information like your IP address, device type, browser, operating system, and pages visited. This data helps us maintain platform security, diagnose technical issues, and understand how our services are used to guide improvements.
  • Voluntary Communications: Any information you provide when contacting our support team via live chat or email is stored to assist you and improve our customer service.

Each piece of data has a defined purpose, directly linked to operating our platform, fulfilling our legal obligations, and enhancing your overall experience.

The essential role of cookies in customizing

Cookies and similar tracking technologies are small files placed on your device that allow us to recognise you and remember your preferences. They play a vital role in making our website function correctly and efficiently.

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for core functions like navigating the site, accessing secure areas (e.g., your account), and processing transactions. The website cannot operate properly without them.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits and error messages. This data is aggregated and used solely to improve how our website works.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember choices you make (such as your language preference or login session) to provide a more personalised and convenient experience during current and future visits.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. However, disabling certain cookies, especially strictly necessary ones, may limit your ability to use some features of our website fully.

How we responsibly use your data to improve our services

We use your personal information responsibly and only for legitimate business purposes:

  1. Service Delivery & Account Management: To create and maintain your account, process your gameplay, facilitate deposits and withdrawals, and provide customer support.
  2. Security & Fraud Prevention: To monitor for and prevent fraudulent activity, money laundering, and other illegal or unauthorized use of our services. This protects both you and our platform.
  3. Legal & Regulatory Compliance: To verify your identity (Know Your Customer checks), maintain responsible gambling controls, and fulfil our reporting obligations under our gaming licence and New Zealand law.
  4. Service Improvement: To analyse usage trends, test new features, and fix technical issues, ensuring our platform remains stable, fast, and user-friendly.
  5. Personalised Communication: With your consent where required, to send you information about promotions, bonuses, and new games that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

Strict protocols for sharing data with trusted partners

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only with trusted third-party service providers under strict contractual agreements that mandate they protect your information and use it solely for the services they provide to us. These partners include:

  • Payment Processors: Companies that handle financial transactions securely (e.g., POLi, Skrill, banks).
  • Software & Platform Providers: Such as Microgaming, for game delivery and random number generation auditing.
  • Professional Advisors: Legal, auditing, and consulting firms bound by confidentiality.
  • Regulatory & Law Enforcement Bodies: Only when required by law, a court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of, our users, or the public.

Any international transfer of data is conducted with appropriate safeguards in place to ensure it receives an equivalent level of protection.

Your rights to access, modify, or delete your data anytime

You have significant rights regarding your personal information. You can exercise these by contacting our Data Protection Officer via our support channels.

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal obligations we must retain (e.g., transaction history for regulatory purposes).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to temporarily stop processing your data under specific circumstances.
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing, such as direct marketing.
  • Right to Data Portability: Where applicable, you can request a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable copy of your data to transfer to another service.

We will respond to all legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe, in accordance with applicable law.

We encourage you to review this policy occasionally to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of our services after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please do not hesitate to contact our support team. Protecting your privacy is our ongoing commitment.
